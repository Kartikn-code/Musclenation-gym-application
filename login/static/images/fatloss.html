Effective Tips to Lose Belly Fat

1. Eat plenty of soluble fiber

Soluble fiber absorbs water and forms a gel that helps slow down food as it passes through your digestive system.

Excellent sources of soluble fiber include

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Legumes
  • Oats
  • Barley

2.Avoid foods that contain trans fats


Trans fats are created by pumping hydrogen into unsaturated fats, such as soybean oil. These fats have been linked to inflammation, heart disease, insulin resistance, and abdominal fat gain in observational and animal studies

3.Moderate your alcohol intake

Alcohol can have health benefits in small amounts, but it can be harmful if you drink too much.
Research suggests that too much alcohol can contribute to belly fat.
